Research Interests

I am interested in General Purpose Computing on Graphics Processing Units (GPGPU). The goal is to accelerate applications by exploring different levels of parallelisms and leveraging the hardware capabilities. Building analytical and empirical models for GPGPU applications can provide guidance for workload scheduling and insights for hardware design. High Performance Computing is challenging due to many factors, including communications and load balancing.
